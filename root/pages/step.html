1. How to use the Online Editor to change FreeBSD document

  1. a. You find problem in a certain page on FreeBSD document.
  2. b. Click "Edit Page" button in the page and going to "FreeBSD Online Document Editor".
  3. c. Change source code(.adoc) in left section. Click "Patch Download" button to download the patch about your change.
  4. d. Click "FreeBSD Bugzilla" button, going FreeBSD Bugzilla to submit the patch.
